Overview

Our organization’s purpose is to unite Black, POC, and white residents that want to change racially systemic and social injustices in our nation’s institutions. We will achieve our goals by using Black Economic Power, mobilizing our community, and being a hub for all things pro-black and anti-racist. We will provide resources for voter education, advocate for black businesses, artists, independent contractors, and allies. It is time for a smart, resourceful, and tactful change.
